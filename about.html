Biography

Jesús Rubio is a Theoretical Physicist and a Surrey Future Fellow. Following his studies for the Spanish Baccalaureate, he completed a Licenciatura degree at the Complutense University of Madrid and a MSc at the Institute for Theoretical Physics of Madrid, specialising in particle physics, cosmology and quantum information theory. He was then awarded a SEPnet scholarship to study for a doctorate at the University of Sussex, under the supervision of Jacob Dunningham. Upon successful completion, he joined the group of Janet Anders at the University of Exeter as a postdoc. In 2022 he was awarded a Surrey Future Fellowship and is now based at the University of Surrey. Jesús's research on quantum sensing, metrology and estimation aims to exploit quantum technologies as a new framework to formulate and answer fundamental questions in physics.
